ABOUT US


Us researchers have embarked on an ambitious journey to delve into the realm of Problem-Based Learning (PBL) in the context of senior high school education. Our collaborative project aims to revolutionize the educational landscape by integrating innovative PBL techniques into the curriculum, ultimately enhancing the learning experience for high school students. With a passion for education and a thirst for innovation, these students are driven by the belief that PBL can empower students to become critical thinkers and problem solvers, equipping them with the skills they need for success in the modern world.
